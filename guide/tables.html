--hiq-table-head-border-width

--hiq-table-caption-padding-vertical
--table-caption-padding-vertical		 Sets the top and bottom padding on the caption element with a table.
--hiq-table-caption-text-color

--hiq-table-caption-font-weight

# Captions

A <caption> functions like a heading for a table. It helps users with screen readers to find a table and understand what it’s about and decide if they want to read it.

HiQ places captions after tables by default.
